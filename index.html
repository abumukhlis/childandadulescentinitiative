Intervention Areas

Education and Empowerment

Our education and empowerment program aim to build knowledge and skills and promote resilience, employments readiness, capacity building and livelihood among young persons’ particularly girls and young women. We thrive to equip them with information, skills and support network so that they can be change agents in their own lives.

Maternal and Child health

We believed that every pregnancy and birth is unique and by addressing inequalities that affect health outcomes, especially sexual and reproductive health and right and gender, is most fundamental to ensuring all women have access to respectful and high maternal care.

Child and adolescents initiative is committed to improving women and children’s health by increasing access, availability, and utilization of key health services through increase utilization of quality family planning, maternal, neonatal, improve nutrition and child health services to address the unmet need of poor and hard-to-reach communities to enable sustainability.

Child Right Promotion

Armed conflict, child marriage, child labor, female genital mutilation and sexual exploitation of children can have long lasting effect on the psychological and well being of children. This can also leads to separation from their families and denial access to education and other essential services.

Child and adolescent initiative is committed to working with children, their families, immediate communities and local authorizes to prevent child neglect, abuse and violence against children by advocating and providing unimpeded access to essential services, including medical and psychosocial support, education and livelihood opportunities that met the needs of children and adolescents.

Water, Sanitation and Hygiene (WASH)

We believed that clean water, basic toilets and good hygiene practice are essential for the survival and development of children. According to UNICEF there are around 2.4 billion people who do not use improved sanitation and 663 million who do not have access to improved water resource especially in rural areas. Without these basic needs, the lives of children are at risk of water and sanitation related diseases which are one of the leading causes of death among Under-five children.

In pursuance of the sustainable development goal to ‘ensure the availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all’’ we work to fulfill the right of children and reach out to the most vulnerable and disadvantaged children.

Our Core Values

INTEGRITY

We are committed to display transparency and honest with ourselves and other people and to all organization within and without our establishment.

PROFESSIONALISM

We strive to be a learning organization with continuous improvement through the delivery of quality and high standard performance in an epic ways.

Who We Are

Child and Adolescents Initiative is a Non-Governmental Organization founded in 2020. We are committed to improving the health and wellbeing of children and adolescents, through the provision of universal access to quality education and healthcare services by harnessing community resources and capacity for sustainable development in Africa. We look forward to partnership with organizations and policy makers who shares our vision of putting smiles on the faces of Young persons in Africa.
